Amit Shah

Biography

Amit Shah, born on October 22, 1964, is an Indian politician and the 32nd Home Minister of India. He is a close aide to Prime Minister Narendra Modi and has played a crucial role in BJP's electoral successes.

Achievements

Amit Shah has been instrumental in several key policy initiatives, including:

  • Leading the BJP to electoral success
  • Expanding BJP's membership
  • Abolition of Triple Talaq
  • Repealing Article 370
  • Passing the Citizenship (Amendment) Bill
